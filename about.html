About

Off the clock.

The short version: an ECE student who likes building things that talk back. The longer version is below.

I'm Dillen a sophomore studying Electrical & Computer Engineering at UNC Charlotte, based in the Charlotte, NC area. I'm building toward a career in Embedded Systems and Hardware engineering.

It started with basic Arduino LED projects and MicroPython on a Raspberry Pi Pico, and has grown into full builds like an ESP32-based alarm system and a hardware reaction timer. I document all of it on GitHub, partly as a portfolio and partly because if I dont write it down I'll forget what I built.

Outside of engineering, I love to be outside and play sports with my friends such as soccer and pickleball. I love to listen to music two of my favorite artists are Drake and Pz'. I also love to play video games and sink alot of my free time into rocket league im currently Champ 1.
